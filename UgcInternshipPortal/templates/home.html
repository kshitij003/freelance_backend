How It Works

  1. Upload: Upload your internship certificate (image, PDF, or DOCX) or paste the text
  2. Extract: AI automatically extracts student name, dates, organization, hours, and more
  3. Auto-fill: Form is pre-filled with extracted data (with confidence indicators)
  4. Verify: Review and edit any low-confidence fields
  5. Submit: System runs CEESCM tokenization and WMD matching
  6. Evaluate: Credits calculated based on curriculum equivalency
  7. ABC Push: Auto-push to ABC if eligible (or mentor review if needed)
